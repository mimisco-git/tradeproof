THE PROBLEM

African SMEs are invisible to global capital

The $500B+ African SME financing gap is not a lack of money. It is a lack of trusted, verifiable trade data. Banks will not lend because they cannot see the ground truth of a transaction.

01
NO CREDIT HISTORY
African SMEs have no machine-readable payment history that banks trust. Every financing application starts from zero regardless of years of trading.
02
SLOW PAYMENTS
Cross-border supplier payments take 3 to 7 business days and cost 5 to 10% in fees. Capital sits idle while businesses wait for wire transfer confirmations.
03
MANUAL TRUST
Procurement runs on phone calls, paper receipts, and personal relationships. Disputes are common, fraud is rampant, and reconciliation is a nightmare.
THE SOLUTION

Programmable procurement.
Verifiable trust.

TradeProof replaces manual trust with onchain proof. Every procurement event becomes an immutable, auditable record on Arc.

01
CREATE A SMART PURCHASE ORDER
A buyer creates a Purchase Order onchain with item description, supplier wallet address, USDC amount, and delivery condition. The PO is recorded permanently on Arc the moment it is created.
ARC + USDC
02
USDC LOCKS IN ESCROW ON ARC
The buyer funds the PO. USDC is locked in the TradeProof escrow contract on Arc. While waiting for delivery, idle capital earns 4.75%+ yield in USYC. The supplier knows payment is guaranteed.
USDC + USYC YIELD
03
AI AGENT MONITORS AND EXECUTES
The TradeProof Procurement Agent monitors delivery conditions continuously. When conditions are met, the agent autonomously releases USDC to the supplier in under one second. No wire transfer. No delay. No dispute about whether payment was sent.
AI AGENT + ARC FINALITY
04
TRADEPROOF SCORE UPDATES
Every completed settlement increments the TradeProof Score for both buyer and supplier. After 5, 20, or 50 completed trades, a business earns Bronze, Silver, or Gold credit status. This onchain credit profile is queryable by any bank or lender via a single API call.
