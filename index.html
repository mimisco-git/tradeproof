The Problem

African SMEs are invisible to global capital

The $500B+ African SME financing gap is not a lack of money. It is a lack of trusted, verifiable trade data. Banks will not lend because they cannot see the ground truth of a transaction.

01
NO CREDIT HISTORY
African SMEs have no machine-readable payment history that banks trust. Every financing application starts from zero regardless of years of trading activity.
02
SLOW, EXPENSIVE PAYMENTS
Cross-border supplier payments take 3 to 7 business days and cost 5 to 10% in fees. Capital sits idle while businesses wait for wire transfer confirmations.
03
MANUAL TRUST
Procurement runs on phone calls, paper receipts, and personal relationships. Disputes are common, fraud is rampant, and reconciliation is a nightmare.
The Solution

Programmable procurement.
Verifiable trust.

TradeProof replaces manual trust with onchain proof. Every procurement event becomes an immutable, auditable record on Arc.

01
CREATE A SMART PURCHASE ORDER
A buyer creates a Purchase Order onchain with item description, supplier wallet, USDC amount, and delivery condition. The PO is recorded permanently on Arc the moment it is created. No paper. No email. No dispute about what was agreed.
ArcUSDC
02
USDC LOCKS IN ESCROW. IDLE CAPITAL EARNS YIELD.
The buyer funds the PO. USDC is locked in the TradeProof escrow contract on Arc. While awaiting delivery confirmation, idle capital is deployed into USYC and earns 4.75% APR. The supplier knows payment is guaranteed. The buyer earns while they wait.
USDCUSYC 4.75%Arc Escrow
03
AI AGENT MONITORS AND EXECUTES
The TradeProof Procurement Agent monitors delivery conditions continuously. When conditions are met, the agent autonomously releases USDC to the supplier on Arc in under one second. No wire transfer. No delay. No dispute about whether payment was sent. The settlement is the proof.

04
TRADEPROOF SCORE UPDATES. CREDIT UNLOCKED.
Every completed settlement increments the TradeProof Score for both buyer and supplier. After 5, 20, or 50 completed trades, a business earns Bronze, Silver, or Gold status. Banks and lenders query this onchain credit profile via API to make credit decisions backed by real, immutable trade data. African SMEs become creditworthy counterparties for the first time.
